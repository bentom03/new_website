About me

I am a UX-UI Design Lead, specializing in developing user interfaces, information architecture, control flows, and aesthetic visuals for web and mobile apps. My design methodology begins with understanding the brief as thoroughly as possible. Inspired by LEAN UX methodology, I try to keep the designs grounded in strong design fundamentals as well as fluidic for client iterations. My focus is and always has been to prepare re-usable code or design to keep the application minimal. Armed with knowledge in design principles and coding frameworks, I am able to provide multiple solutions to a single problem.

I try to lead by example, and my insatiable thirst for knowledge keeps me regularly upgrading my skills. Aside from this, I put strong emphasis on communication skills as well as documentation part of the process.
