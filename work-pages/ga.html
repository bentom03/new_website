BCDS' Glimpse Analytix

Canada's child development and behavioral studies group wanted a web & mobile tool to connect parents to doctors and mentors as they move through the child's journey into the future.

The Brief

Another huge project, still in development, even as I have moved away from it. However, the user interface and experience design phase were completed by me. This project was to be on web as well as mobile based. The Glimpse Analytix mobile app was a supposedly a native app. Everything was to be prepared from scratch. The company had no branding for the product. Therefore, our fist task was to work on a logo and thereafter a style guide and colour options. A lot of forms were to be incorporated within the design. Once of client's many important requests was to have a form builder for custom forms that users or mentors can make. The initial wireframes (developed in Balsimiq) for this extensive project are available here to view.

Design

After the approval of the wireframes, the client did not wish to see a low-fidelity prototype. Full fledged design work was to be commenced and carried on so as to finish the phase on time.Some of the web portal designs can be seen above. Stark with enough white space directing the eye of the user to relevant information. Separate sections were color coded for ease of understanding and consistency within departments.

App design phase began after the approval of the logo and colour scheme. Typeface selected was Gotham for its geometric appearance and large x-height. Clarity was of prime importance as we were venturing in the field of medicine. Since we were dealing the field of psychiatry, the colour scheme chosen for the app was light so as to inspire transquility.

Once all designs were approved, development phase began. To make tables and forms, Telerik was used for this project.
