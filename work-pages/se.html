Sweatequite, USA

Sweatequite matches accomplished professionals and advanced students (Grinders) to non-traditional opportunities at start-ups, small businesses, and non-profits. They also assist in finding a second stream of income without having to quit the day job. Work non-traditional hours.

The Brief

Sweatequite, was the company's first client from United States of America. Their project was to connect the skilled workforce to the employers through this website. After discussion and deliberations, we ended up agreeing on a single page website where all the content was available, however, after sign up, the user would be taken to a different area of the website specifically tailored for a grinder or a business, depending upon their login information.

A major requirement was that the website should have resume builders for grinders that would take in extensive personal information and it would be a long process for the user.

Design

From the requirements, we gauged that there would be a lot of forms. The user experience should be such that the design should not inimidate the user and keep them calm while they enter the requirement information.

Thus, we decided to keep light colours and clear large fonts. A combination of grey and white was employed, which produces a calming effect on users along with blue that inspires trust in the user's mind (inspired by facebook's colour schemes). The users needed to know that their data was safe on the web with Sweatequite. Check out the current website here.
