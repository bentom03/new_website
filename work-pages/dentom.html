Denny Thomas Portfolio

Denny Thomas is American Cinema Editor's 'Eddie' award winner and Emmy nominated editor on the critically acclaimed, award winning series Vice on HBO.

The Brief

After winning the American Cinema Editor's award, Denny Thomas was in need of a website showcasing his extraordinary work. The emmy nominated editor has worked on countless critically acclaimed projects and documentary. His requirements were simple: a website to show his work and it should have a link to his LinkedIn profile and IMDB profile. The videos were to be linked with You Tube to keep the website light.

Design

This was my first freelance project. In order to get it right, several ideas were discussed and samples shown. My suggestion was to keep a carousel to highlight select videos with a play button on each. Clicking on the button will take users to a seperate page where an embedded YouTube console will play the video. Along with this view, there was to be incorporated a tile view for easier access to the videos.

This idea was accepted and any feedback received till date from colleagues and peers have been positive. Check out the current website here.
