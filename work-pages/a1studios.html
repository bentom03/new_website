A1 Design Studios

A1 Design Studios, is a Canadian company that provides building designs, interior designs, and undertakes construction management. The team provides designs that keep property fresh and maintainable. They add excitement and market appeal to the property, whether it is in the economy sector, midscale, or upscale market.

The Brief

After the merger of Petra Hygienic Systems and A-1 Textiles, our agency was brough in to do the web development for all their subsidiaries. It included a new website for PetrA-1 and A-1 Design Studios. Being unresponsive and incompatible with many browsers and mobile devices, the existing website was in need of an overhauling. A fresh look was to be rendered and it had to be responsive.

Research was provided to us by the client. Their briefing was quite thorough and after studying it, we began the design phase the very next day.

Design

It is my only project, where we did not receive any change requests whatsoever. The website was developed exactly as we had pitched. Even most of the images selected were approved. We approached it from singular perspective: to keep the design simple and efficient. The layout included a hero image with relevant information, custom icons with copy emphasising on the service specialisation and footer with contact information.

The design was immediately approved and pushed into phase 2 — high fidelity prototype. We selected appropriate imagery from deposit photos and got approval for the final designs. Within a wekk, the website was tested and by the next week, it was live. Check it out here.
