The Brief

Center for Civil Society required an independent website connecting students, alumni, and faculty in one place. The new website — CCS Academy — was to include program details, student downloadable materials, event details and testimonials. From the beginning they insisted that the website be made in such a way that it can be accessed and amended according to the need of the hour by the CCS team.

This was the longest running project that I have ever encountered. The reasons were simple: The CCS team handling this project shuffled three times. Naturally, with every change in team members, we had to prepare designs accordingly.

Design

After the third iteration, this design was approved. The home page was designed as a single scroller with many carousals. It kept getting complicated as we delved deeper into the project. Repeated requests for enhancements delayed the progress quite a lot.

In hindsight, I believe a wordpress theme would have worked better for their requirements and it would have been an easier interface for the CCS team to get trained on. Check out the current website here.
