Gyrox Aviation

Gyrox aviation — making an entry into the aviation sector with their all new Gyroplane: The future of air commute. It is neither a plane nor a helicopter; it does not stall and can operate from sea or land. The company is pioneering the distribution, assembly and manufacture of Gyroplanes in India since this aerial platform provides immense utility and versatility with multifaceted applications.

The Brief

We were really excited to have this project — first from an aviation industry. The client wanted a stunning website to launch their product and begin operation within the year. We offered thema high-fidelity prototype that indeed did the job. However, due to budgetary constraints the project was taken away from us.

Design

Our prototype included a video playing at the top of the single page home screen. The users would find the rest of the plane related information and images as they scroll down. At the bottom was a form that would allow users to request for a demo flight.
